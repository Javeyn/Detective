The year is 198X. Welcome to New Jet City.
You are a detective at the 11th police precinct in New Jet City. Your most recent cases have had you deep undercover. Deep enough that many of your fellow officers question your integrity as a police officer. Look, you've gone through some crazy things, but your a good cop. I mean, you are a good cop....right?
You've recently been working a case involving a club in South NJC. An influx of high power firearms has made its way into the city, and violent crimes have skyrocketed. You suspect that the club owner is involved, a man who goes by the name of Mr Don. Mr. Don has been tied to organized crime all throughout the city.
A reliable source down at the South NJC Port has contacted you about rumors of large arms purchases. At 11pm tomorrow night, a large container ship will be arriving. On this ship will be a containers with over $500,000 dollars worth of large firearms and ammunition. Coincidentally, Mr. Don has a container on this very same ship, shipping 3 "large antique vehicles".
You and your partner go to your boss to pitch the idea, and hes less than impressed. "Last time I went out on a limb for you two, I fell flat on my face. This better pan out." he warns you. You let him know there is no need to worry. The source has provided you with a manifest of the shipping container. The port is expecting a delivery of 6 large containers, but the manifest seems to not account for 2 of the containers, including Mr. Don's.
The ship arrives at 11:18pm, and the raid is flawlessly executed, with one glaring exception. When all the dust finally settles, there are no guns, and there are no antique cars. You've been duped again.
The next day your boss really lets you two have it. There was a lot of yelling at him by his boss, so of course, there is going to be a lot of yelling directed at you. You decide it is probably best if you lay low for a while. Except...
Later that day your source from the pier calls you. At 10:36pm last night, an emergency reroute was called in to divert 2 containers from Port South to Port West. Your guess is that someone in the precinct warned Mr. Don about the raid, and he was able to get his guns safely moved. You decide to go to Port West to look for clues.
Port West turned out to be a big waste of time, so you head back home. When you walk into your house, you can hear the tail end of a message being left on your message machine. When you press play on the machine, a familiar voice comes through...
"Hey, it's your partner John. There have been some developements in our case, you need to call me at my house. Call me right now." You pick up the phone and call John. "I'm glad you called," He starts," I think I found one of the sellers for the guns. I am going to try to buy some, and I need you to be my backup."
Its almost too good to be true. You grab your pistol, your badge, and your jacket, and you head out the door.
